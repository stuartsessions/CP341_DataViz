Jersey Numbers and their Positions September 1, 2009 | (0) Comments


I noticed when watching games that the same numbers tend to appear in the same positions. For example, forwards are often seen wearing numbers 7-11, while goalkeepers either wear 0/1, or a random high number. Having noticed this, I expected to see the different positions show different jersey number data.

This phenomonon is likely due to idols and influence across generations. For example, famous footballer Lionel Messi wears number 10, so people who look up to him and likely play attack will also want to wear number 10.
