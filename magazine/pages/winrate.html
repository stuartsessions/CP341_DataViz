Team Winrates


It makes sense that the teams that win more, have players with higher winrates. This dataset includes players' past statistics from the premier league, which could contain other teams. Therefore, this is not a completely accurate representation of each team's success. However, we still generally see that the more successful teams have higher player average winrates.
